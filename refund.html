All purchases are processed by Paddle as the Merchant of Record. You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase — no questions asked. EU/UK customers have a statutory right to a full refund within 14 days. After 14 days, refunds are handled case-by-case. Just email us and we'll sort it out.

All payments on YourAnimal are processed by Paddle.com, who acts as the Merchant of Record for all transactions. This means Paddle handles billing, invoicing, tax collection, and — where applicable — refunds on our behalf.

By purchasing on YourAnimal, you agree to Paddle's Privacy Policy and Paddle's Buyer Terms in addition to our own policies.

2. 14-Day Refund Window

We offer a 14-day refund window from the date of purchase for all paid products:

To request a refund within 14 days, email us at kunha0303@gmail.com with your purchase email and date. We'll process it promptly — no hassle.

EU & UK customers — statutory right

Under the EU Consumer Rights Directive and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations, you have a statutory right to cancel and receive a full refund within 14 days of purchase, even for digital content — unless you explicitly consented to immediate delivery and acknowledged that your right to cancel is lost upon accessing the content. If you accessed the content immediately, your statutory right may have been waived at checkout. Contact us either way and we will assess your request fairly.

3. After 14 Days

After the 14-day window, we review refund requests on a case-by-case basis. We will always make things right if:

  • You were charged but couldn't access the content — payment went through but the feature didn't unlock.
  • You were charged twice — a duplicate charge for the same purchase.
  • A technical error on our side prevented you from receiving what you paid for.

Contact us at kunha0303@gmail.com and include your purchase email, the date of purchase, and a brief description of the issue.

4. Subscriptions

The All-In-One subscription ($9.99/month) can be cancelled at any time. When you cancel:

  • You retain full access until the end of the current billing period.
  • You will not be charged again.
  • Refunds for the current period are available within the 14-day window from the most recent charge.

Tip: try before you pay

Your #1 pet match result and one Name Generator use are completely free — no card required. We encourage you to explore the free features so you can be confident before purchasing.

5. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us at kunha0303@gmail.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We respond within 1 business day and can almost always resolve things directly and quickly.
