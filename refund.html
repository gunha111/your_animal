💳 Refunds

Refund Policy

Honest, fair, and straightforward — because that's how we like to do things.

Company: YourAnimal (mypetgenerator.com)
Last updated: March 2025
Contact: support@mypetgenerator.com

The short version

Try the free features first — your #1 pet match and one Name Generator use are always free. If something breaks on our end and you can't access what you paid for, contact us within 48 hours and we'll make it right. Otherwise, because digital content is delivered instantly, purchases are non-refundable once accessed.

1. Our Approach

We built YourAnimal because we genuinely love helping people find the right pet. That means we want every interaction with us to feel fair and trustworthy — including how we handle money.

We offer a meaningful free tier for exactly this reason: your #1 pet match result, one free Name Generator use, and full community access — all at no cost, no card required. We encourage you to explore the free features before deciding whether to pay for more.

2. Why We Can't Offer Automatic Refunds

When you unlock a paid feature — like your TOP 5 results, the Name Generator, or a Care Plan — that content is generated and displayed to you immediately. There is no physical product, no shipping, no waiting period.

Because the content is digital and delivered the moment you pay, it cannot be "returned" the same way a physical item can. Once you've seen your results, we have no way to un-deliver them. This is standard practice for digital goods.

That's why we say: try before you pay.

Your #1 match result is completely free and gives you a real sense of the quality and depth of our analysis. Only upgrade when you feel confident you'll find value in it.

3. What Is Covered (Exceptions)

While we generally cannot offer refunds for accessed digital content, we will always make things right if something goes wrong on our end. You may be eligible for a refund or credit if:

  • You were charged but couldn't access the content — for example, a payment went through but your results page showed an error, or the feature didn't unlock as expected.
  • You were charged twice — a duplicate charge for the same purchase.
  • A technical error on our side prevented you from receiving what you paid for.

In these cases, please contact us at support@mypetgenerator.com within 48 hours of the purchase. Include your email address, the date and time of purchase, and a brief description of what happened. We'll investigate and resolve it promptly.

4. Subscription Cancellations

The All-In-One subscription ($9.99/month) can be cancelled at any time from your account settings. Cancelling is immediate and stops the next billing cycle.

When you cancel:

  • You keep full access to all subscription features until the end of your current billing period.
  • You will not be charged again after the cancellation date.
  • We do not offer pro-rated refunds for the unused portion of a billing period, as the features remain available to you until it ends.

If you believe you were charged after cancelling, or if your cancellation didn't process correctly, please contact us right away and we'll investigate.

Tip: cancel before your renewal date

Your renewal date is shown in your account settings. To avoid being charged for another month, cancel at least 24 hours before that date. We process cancellations immediately, so there's no delay.

5. What We Don't Refund

To be transparent, we do not issue refunds in the following situations:

  • You changed your mind after accessing your results.
  • You didn't like your pet match recommendation.
  • You forgot to cancel a subscription before the renewal date.
  • You purchased a feature and later realised the free version was enough for you.

This is why we strongly encourage using the free features first. The quiz, your #1 result, and the Name Generator preview give you a genuine feel for the quality of our content before you commit to paying for more.

6. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us at support@mypetgenerator.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We're almost always able to resolve issues quickly and directly.

Fraudulent chargebacks may result in account termination and may be reported to Paddle, our payment processor.

7. How to Contact Us

If you have a question about a charge, believe you qualify for a refund under our exceptions, or just want to talk something through, we're here to help.

  • Email: support@mypetgenerator.com
  • Response time: We aim to reply within 1 business day.
  • Window for technical issues: Contact us within 48 hours of your purchase.

We read every message personally and we genuinely want to help.
