Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What's included in the free plan? +

The free plan gives you full access to the 25-question pet matching quiz, your #1 pet match result with complete analysis, one use of the Name Generator, and unlimited access to the community and blog. No credit card required — ever.

How does the subscription work? +

The All-In-One subscription is billed at $5.99 per month through Paddle, our secure payment processor. You get immediate access to all features. You can cancel at any time from your account settings — no penalty, no questions asked. Cancelling stops the next billing cycle; you keep access until the end of the current period.

Can I use My Pet Generator on multiple devices? +

Yes. My Pet Generator works in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Your account and any unlocked features are tied to your login, so you can access them from any device at any time.

What's the difference between Pay Per Feature and the subscription? +

Pay Per Feature lets you unlock individual items — Name Generator for $1.99, TOP 5 results for $2.99, Care Plan for $2.99. These are permanent, one-time unlocks. The subscription ($5.99/mo) gives you all features unlocked for 30 days and is the better value if you plan to use more than one feature, or want access to new features as they launch.

What payment methods are accepted? +

Payments are handled securely by Paddle. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Paddle is a registered Merchant of Record, which means they handle all sales tax and VAT on our behalf.

Are purchases refundable? +

Because our content is digital and delivered instantly, purchases are generally non-refundable once the content has been accessed. That's why we encourage you to try the free features first — your #1 result and one Name Generator use are always free.
