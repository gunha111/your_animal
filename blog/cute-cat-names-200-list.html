200 Cute Cat Names
The Ultimate List for 2026

⏱ 8 min read · Name Ideas by Theme

Naming a cat is an art form. The name has to feel right when you call it across the apartment at 2am. It has to suit their face, their vibe, their attitude. This list covers 200 names organized by theme — from elegant to absurd.

💡 Cat naming tip: Cats respond best to names with 1–2 syllables that end in a vowel sound — "Luna," "Mochi," "Cleo." They pick up on high-pitched sounds, which is why names ending in "ee" sounds work well.

How to Pick the Right Name

  • Say it out loud 10 times. If it feels natural calling it across a room, it's a good sign.
  • Watch them for 24 hours first. Their personality almost always suggests a name.
  • Avoid names that sound like commands. "Kit" sounds like "Sit." "Bo" sounds like "No." This confuses training.
  • Don't stress too much. Cats often don't care what you call them — they respond to your tone, not the word.
